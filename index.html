Police Clearance Guide

National Police Clearance Guide

Step 1: Register Online

Visit pnpclearance.ph and register (new applicant) or log in (existing applicant). Fill out the required information and set an appointment at a suitable date and preferred Police Station. Take note of the system-generated Reference Number.

Bisitahin ang pnpclearance.ph at magparehistro (bagong aplikante) o mag-log in (rehistradong aplikante). I-fill out ang mga kinakailangang impormasyon at mag-set ng appointment sa petsa at Police Station na gusto mo. Tandaan ang system-generated Reference Number.

Step 2: Settle Payment

Pay online thru Landbank of the Philippines Link.Biz Portal using your account from LandBank, Bancnet or G-Cash. Secure a copy of the Payment Confirmation Slip and Electronic Official Receipt (OR) as proof of payment.

Magbayad online sa pamamagitan ng Land Bank of the Philippines Link.Biz Portal gamit ang iyong account sa LandBank, Bancnet o G-Cash. Kumuha ng kopya ng Payment Confirmation Slip at Electronic Official Receipt (OR) bilang patunay ng pagbabayad.

List of Valid IDs (Listahan ng mga wastong ID)

• PNP ID

• Voter’s ID

• Postal ID

• TIN ID

• GSIS UMID

• LTO Driver’s License

• Senior Citizen’s ID

• OFW ID

Step 3: Police Station Visit

Pumunta sa iyong napiling Police Station sa araw ng iyong appointment.

Dalhin ang mga sumusunod:

  • Reference Number
  • Proof of Payment / Official Receipt (OR)
  • Dalawang (2) Valid ID

Biometrics: Magpakuha ng biometrics (photo, fingerprint, at lagda).

Verification: Maghintay na makumpleto ang proseso ng pag-beripika bago makuha ang iyong National Police Clearance.
